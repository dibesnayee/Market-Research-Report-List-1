<p><strong>The Speech Synthesis Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concatenation Synthesis</li><li>Formant Synthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The speech synthesis software market primarily includes two main types: concatenation synthesis and formant synthesis. Concatenation synthesis uses pre-recorded human speech segments, seamlessly piecing them together to create natural-sounding speech, making it widely popular for applications requiring high-quality audio. In contrast, formant synthesis generates speech by simulating the acoustic properties of human vocal tracts, allowing for more flexible and less resource-intensive synthesis. Each type caters to specific needs, influencing adoption across various industries like gaming, accessibility, and virtual assistants.</p></p>
