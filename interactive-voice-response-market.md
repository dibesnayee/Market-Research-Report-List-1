<p><p>The Interactive Voice Response (IVR) market is divided into two primary types: speech-based and touch-tone based systems. Speech-based IVR allows users to interact using natural language, enabling a more intuitive and personalized experience. In contrast, touch-tone IVR relies on users inputting responses using a keypad, which can be more straightforward but less flexible. Both systems serve to automate customer interactions, streamline processes, and improve efficiency within various industries, such as telecommunications, banking, and healthcare.</p></p>
