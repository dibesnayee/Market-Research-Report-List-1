<p><ul><li>Microfiltration</li><li>Ultrafiltration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC ceramic flat sheet membrane market includes two primary types: microfiltration and ultrafiltration. Microfiltration membranes feature larger pore sizes, typically allowing particles and contaminants of around 0.1 to 10 microns to be filtered out, making them ideal for applications such as wastewater treatment and beverage purification. Ultrafiltration membranes, with smaller pore sizes (0.01 to 0.1 microns), effectively remove colloids, bacteria, and larger organic molecules, serving applications like desalination and pharmaceutical processing. Both membrane types enhance water quality and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
