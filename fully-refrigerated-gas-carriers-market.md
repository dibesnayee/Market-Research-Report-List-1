<p><p>The Fully Refrigerated Gas Carriers market consists of three main types: Individual Tank, Semi-film Tank, and One-piece Tank. Individual Tanks are separate, insulated compartments designed to carry liquefied gases, ensuring minimal contamination. Semi-film Tanks incorporate a flexible membrane, allowing for efficient gas expansion and contraction. One-piece Tanks feature a single, robust structure that provides maximum strength and safety, ideal for large-volume transport. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the cargo requirements, operational efficiency, and safety standards.</p></p>
