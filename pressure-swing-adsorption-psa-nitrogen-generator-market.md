<p><strong>The Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) Nitrogen Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Molecular Sieve Air Separation Nitrogen Production"</li><li>"Membrane Air Separation Nitrogen Production"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) nitrogen generator market is primarily divided into two types: Molecular Sieve Air Separation and Membrane Air Separation. Molecular Sieve Air Separation utilizes adsorbent materials to selectively capture oxygen, yielding high-purity nitrogen, making it suitable for applications requiring stringent purity levels. In contrast, Membrane Air Separation employs selectively permeable membranes to separate gases based on their molecular size, offering a more compact and energy-efficient solution for nitrogen production, ideal for lower purity applications.</p></p>
