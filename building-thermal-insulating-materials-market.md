<p><p>The Building Thermal Insulating Materials market comprises various types that enhance energy efficiency in structures. Rock wool, made from volcanic rock, offers excellent thermal and fire resistance. Glass wool, crafted from recycled glass, provides lightweight insulation with good sound absorption. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) are rigid foam insulations known for moisture resistance and thermal performance. PU (Polyurethane) and PIR (Polyisocyanurate) insulation materials boast high efficiency and versatility. The 'Others' category includes alternative materials like cellulose and sheep's wool.</p></p>
