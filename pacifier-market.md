<p><p>The pacifier market is primarily divided into two main types: liquid silicone pacifiers and natural latex pacifiers. Liquid silicone pacifiers are made from synthetic silicone, offering durability, easy cleaning, and resistance to heat and UV rays, making them a popular choice among parents. In contrast, natural latex pacifiers are derived from rubber tree sap, providing a softer feel and flexibility. While both types serve the same purpose of soothing infants, their material differences cater to various preferences and sensitivities in babies.</p></p>
