<p><p>Drive-In Pallet Racking is a high-density storage solution designed for efficient space utilization in warehouses. It features several market types: </p><p>1. **Duplex Type** allows for both front and back access to pallets, maximizing storage while maintaining accessibility.</p><p>2. **Straight-Through Type** enables direct entry and exit at either end, facilitating seamless loading and retrieval.</p><p>3. **Single Entry Type** provides access from one side only, ideal for environments with limited space and lower inventory turnover. Each type caters to different operational needs and space constraints.</p></p>
