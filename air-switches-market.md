<p><ul><li>Bellows</li><li>Push Button</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air switches market can be categorized into two primary types: bellows and push button switches. Bellows air switches utilize a flexible diaphragm that responds to pressure changes, making them ideal for applications requiring sensitive actuation, such as in HVAC systems. Push button air switches, on the other hand, are activated by physical pressing, offering straightforward usability in various equipment. Both types are prevalent in industrial and consumer settings, ensuring reliable operation in environments that require pneumatic control solutions.</p></p>
