<p><strong>The Expedition Motor Yachts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement Hull</li><li>Semi-Displacement Hull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Expedition motor yachts are categorized into two primary types based on their hull designs: displacement hulls and semi-displacement hulls. Displacement hulls are designed for efficiency, gliding through water at lower speeds and offering stability, making them suitable for long-range cruising in challenging conditions. Conversely, semi-displacement hulls combine features of both displacement and planing hulls, allowing for higher speeds while still maintaining good fuel efficiency and sea-keeping abilities. Each type caters to different cruising preferences and operational conditions in the market.</p></p>
