<p><p>The PVC pipe cement market is categorized into three main types based on viscosity and application. Regular Bodied PVC Pipe Cement is suited for general use, providing a quick bond for small diameter pipes. Medium Bodied PVC Pipe Cement offers a thicker consistency for standard pipes, allowing for better gap filling. Heavy Bodied PVC Pipe Cement is designed for larger diameter pipes, providing a strong, durable bond that withstands high pressures, ensuring reliability in critical plumbing and industrial applications.</p></p>
