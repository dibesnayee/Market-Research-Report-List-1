<p><p>Scrubber mercury control systems are vital for reducing mercury emissions from industrial processes. The market is divided into two main types: dry control systems and wet control systems. </p><p>Dry control systems typically use sorbents to capture mercury from flue gas without water, making them suitable for facilities with limited water access. In contrast, wet control systems involve scrubbing flue gas with a liquid solution, effectively dissolving and removing mercury. Both systems are essential for complying with environmental regulations and improving air quality.</p></p>
