<p><ul><li>Dual Channel Testing</li><li>Multi Channel Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMG test system market is categorized into dual-channel and multi-channel testing. Dual-channel testing involves monitoring two muscles simultaneously, allowing for straightforward analysis of neuromuscular function. In contrast, multi-channel testing enables the assessment of multiple muscles at once, providing a comprehensive view of muscle activity and coordination. This multi-faceted approach is especially useful in complex diagnostic scenarios and offers enhanced data for research and clinical applications, catering to diverse needs within neurology and rehabilitation fields.</p></p>
