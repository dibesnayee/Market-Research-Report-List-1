<p><p>The waste incinerators market comprises several types, including rotary kiln, fluidized bed, static hearth, and others. Rotary kilns utilize a rotating cylinder to efficiently burn waste at high temperatures. Fluidized bed incinerators use air to suspend waste particles, enhancing combustion efficiency. Static hearth incinerators involve a fixed bed where waste is burned in place. Other types include modular systems and mobile incinerators, each designed for specific waste types and operational needs, addressing environmental regulations and energy recovery.</p></p>
