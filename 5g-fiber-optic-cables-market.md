<p><strong>The 5G Fiber Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Cable</li><li>Multi-Mode Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G fiber optic cables market is primarily divided into two types: single-mode and multi-mode cables. Single-mode cables use a single light path, allowing for long-distance transmission with higher bandwidth and lower signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-speed internet. Multi-mode cables, on the other hand, feature multiple light paths, supporting shorter distances with higher capacity, suitable for local area networks and data centers. Both types play critical roles in enhancing 5G network performance and scalability.</p></p>
