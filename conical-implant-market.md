<p><ul><li>Root Implant</li><li>Plate Dental Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conical implant market is primarily categorized into two types: root implants and plate dental implants. Root implants are designed to mimic the natural tooth root, providing stability and support for crowns or bridges, making them ideal for single-tooth replacements. In contrast, plate dental implants are flat and wide, suitable for patients with insufficient bone height, offering a broader surface area for enhanced stability. Both types ensure effective integration with the jawbone and improved oral function.</p></p>
