<p><ul><li>Right-angle Gearboxes</li><li>In-line Gearboxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low backlash planetary gearboxes market is categorized into two main types: right-angle gearboxes and in-line gearboxes. Right-angle gearboxes are designed to transmit motion at a 90-degree angle, making them ideal for applications with space constraints. In contrast, in-line gearboxes maintain a straight alignment between the input and output shafts, offering higher efficiency and compact design for direct coupling. Both types are crucial in precision motion control applications, providing enhanced torque transmission and minimal backlash for reliable performance.</p></p>
