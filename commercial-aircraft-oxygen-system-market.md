<p><strong>The Commercial Aircraft Oxygen System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Storage System</li><li>Delivery System</li><li>Cannula</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Aircraft Oxygen System market encompasses three primary types: storage systems, delivery systems, and cannulas. Storage systems refer to the tanks or containers that hold the oxygen, typically configured as high-pressure cylinders or chemical oxygen generators. Delivery systems involve the mechanisms that transport oxygen from the storage source to passengers and crew, often utilizing masks or tubing. Cannulas are specialized devices that allow for nasal delivery of oxygen, providing an alternative means of administration, enhancing comfort, and efficiency during emergencies.</p></p>
