Vacuum grippers employ suction cups, perfect for handling flat, smooth surfaces like glass or metal. Magnetic grippers utilize magnetic forces to securely hold ferrous materials, making them effective in metalworking environments. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, enhancing automation efficiency.</p></p>
