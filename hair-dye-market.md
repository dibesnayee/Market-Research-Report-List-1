<p><ul><li>Temporary Hair Dye</li><li>Semi- & Demi-Permanent Hair Dye</li><li>Permanent Hair Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair dye market comprises three main types: Temporary, Semi- & Demi-Permanent, and Permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide short-lived color that washes out in a few shampoos, ideal for special occasions. Semi-Permanent dyes last longer, typically up to several weeks, gradually fading with washes. Demi-Permanent dyes offer a balance, lasting longer than semis but without the depth of permanent dyes. Permanent hair dyes provide long-lasting color that penetrates the hair shaft, providing enduring coverage, often requiring regrowth maintenance.</p></p>
