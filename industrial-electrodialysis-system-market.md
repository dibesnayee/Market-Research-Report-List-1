<p><p>The industrial electrodialysis system market is primarily categorized into continuous and batch electrodialysis types. Continuous electrodialysis involves a constant flow of feed solutions, facilitating ongoing separation processes, ideal for large-scale operations. In contrast, batch electrodialysis treats discrete volumes of feed solutions, making it suitable for smaller-scale applications or specific process requirements. Both systems leverage ion-exchange membranes to separate ions, enhancing the efficiency of water desalination, wastewater treatment, and recovery of valuable materials in various industries.</p></p>
