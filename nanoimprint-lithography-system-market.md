<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Nanoimprint Lithography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Embossing (HE)</li><li>UV-based Nanoimprint Lithography (UV-NIL)</li><li>Micro Contact Printing (µ-CP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nanoimprint Lithography System market consists of three key types: Hot Embossing (HE), UV-based Nanoimprint Lithography (UV-NIL), and Micro Contact Printing (µ-CP). </p><p>Hot Embossing involves heating a mold to imprint nanoscale patterns onto substrates, ideal for high-resolution features. UV-NIL uses ultraviolet light to cure a polymer film after imprinting, allowing for faster processing and reusable molds. Micro Contact Printing (µ-CP) transfers nanoscale patterns via an elastomeric stamp, suitable for flexible substrates and biomolecular applications, enhancing versatility in nanofabrication.</p></p>
