<p><p>The cancer immunotherapy drugs market comprises various types, including monoclonal antibodies, checkpoint inhibitors, interferons, and interleukins. Monoclonal antibodies selectively target cancer cells, enhancing immune response. Checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that hinder immune activation, allowing T-cells to attack tumors more effectively. Interferons are cytokines that boost immune response and inhibit tumor growth, while interleukins promote the proliferation and activation of immune cells. Together, these therapies represent innovative approaches that harness the body's immune system to combat cancer more effectively.</p></p>
