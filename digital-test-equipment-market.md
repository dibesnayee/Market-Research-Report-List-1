Digital Multimeters (DMM) measure voltage, current, and resistance, making them essential for general electrical testing. Digital LCR Meters focus on measuring inductance (L), capacitance (C), and resistance (R), ideal for assessing passive components. Digital Oscilloscopes capture and visualize electrical signals over time, aiding in waveform analysis. Other test equipment includes signal generators and spectrum analyzers, broadening the scope for specialized electronics testing and development.</p></p>
