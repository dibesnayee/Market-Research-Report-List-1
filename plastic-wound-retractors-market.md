<p><p>The plastic wound retractors market is primarily categorized into two types: ring-based and prong-based. Ring-based retractors feature a circular design that provides broad exposure of the surgical site, ensuring minimal tissue trauma during procedures. In contrast, prong-based retractors utilize pointed extensions that anchor into the tissue, allowing for more precise retraction in confined spaces. Both types enhance visibility and access during surgeries, catering to different surgical needs and preferences, and are essential for improving operational efficiency in medical settings.</p></p>
