<p><p>The radial tire market is divided into three main types: steel radial tires, semi-steel radial tires, and full-fiber radial tires. Steel radial tires utilize steel belts for superior strength and durability, enhancing performance and longevity. Semi-steel radial tires combine steel and fabric, offering a balanced durability and flexibility. Full-fiber radial tires are made entirely from fiber materials, providing lightweight and economical options, which are often favored in lower-performance applications. Each type caters to different vehicle needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
