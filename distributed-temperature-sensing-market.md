<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multipoint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) technology is classified into two primary market types: single-point and multipoint sensing. Single-point sensors offer temperature measurements at a specific location, ideal for applications needing focused monitoring, like equipment hotspots. In contrast, multipoint sensors provide temperature data across a range of locations along a fiber optic cable, enabling comprehensive thermal profiles in applications such as pipeline monitoring and environmental studies. Each type caters to different needs, from precise monitoring to extensive coverage.</p></p>
