<p><p>The ophthalmic femtosecond lasers market is divided into two main types: full femtosecond and half femtosecond lasers. Full femtosecond lasers are designed for comprehensive eye surgeries, providing precise, controlled cutting and reducing complications during procedures like cataract surgery and LASIK. In contrast, half femtosecond lasers utilize a complementary approach, combining femtosecond technology with traditional methods to enhance surgical outcomes while being cost-effective. Both types aim to improve patient safety, reduce recovery times, and enhance surgical accuracy.</p></p>
