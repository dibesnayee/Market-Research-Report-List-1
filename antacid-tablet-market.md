<p><p>The antacid tablet market comprises three primary types: calcium-based, magnesium-based, and mixed antacid tablets. Calcium-based antacids, such as calcium carbonate, provide quick relief from heartburn and indigestion while also contributing to dietary calcium. Magnesium-based antacids, like magnesium hydroxide, effectively neutralize stomach acid and may offer a laxative effect. Mixed antacid tablets combine calcium and magnesium, balancing the benefits of both, and often include other ingredients for enhanced efficacy, catering to diverse consumer preferences in digestive health.</p></p>
