<p><strong>The Industrial Resin 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LFS Technology</li><li>FDM Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial resin 3D printer market encompasses various technologies, primarily LFS (Low Force Stereolithography) and FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling). LFS technology utilizes a flexible film and UV light to produce high-resolution prints with minimal force, enabling fine details and faster production times. In contrast, FDM technology involves extruding molten thermoplastic filaments layer by layer to create robust parts, often used for prototyping and functional applications. Both technologies cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing precision and efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
