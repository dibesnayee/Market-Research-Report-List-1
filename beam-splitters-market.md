<p><p>The beam splitters market consists of three main types: plate type, pellicle type, and cube type. Plate beam splitters are flat optical devices that reflect and transmit light at specific ratios, commonly used in laboratory settings. Pellicle beam splitters are thin membranes providing minimal optical distortion, ideal for high-precision applications. Cube beam splitters consist of two prisms glued together, offering compactness and high durability, making them suitable for various optical systems. Each type serves distinct needs in optical applications.</p></p>
