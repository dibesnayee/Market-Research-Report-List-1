<p><strong>The Baby Mattresses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Foam</li><li>Innerspring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baby mattresses market comprises two primary types: foam and innerspring. Foam mattresses are lightweight, offer excellent contouring support, and are often made from memory foam or high-density foam, providing comfort and reducing pressure points for infants. In contrast, innerspring mattresses use a system of coils, offering durability and better airflow, which helps regulate temperature. Each type has distinct benefits, catering to parents' preferences for safety, comfort, and support as they choose the best sleeping solution for their babies.</p></p>
