<p><p>The dog treadmill market is categorized into two primary types: electric and manual. Electric dog treadmills operate on motorized systems, offering adjustable speeds and incline settings, making them suitable for various dog sizes and fitness levels. They often include digital displays for tracking performance. In contrast, manual dog treadmills rely on the dog's movement to propel the belt, encouraging natural walking or running. These are typically more affordable and portable but may require more effort from the dog to use effectively.</p></p>
