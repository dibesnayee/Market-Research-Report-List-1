<p><p>The paint spray guns market comprises various types, including High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP) and Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) systems. HVLP guns deliver a large volume of paint at lower pressure, promoting better atomization and reducing overspray, making them ideal for detailed work. LVLP guns use less air volume, producing finer finishes while minimizing paint waste, suitable for smaller projects. Other types may include airless and electrostatic spray systems, catering to diverse applications across automotive, construction, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
