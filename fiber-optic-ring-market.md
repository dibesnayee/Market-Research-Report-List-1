<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optic Ring</li><li>Multimode Fiber Optic Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optic Ring Market comprises two primary types: Single-Mode Fiber Optic Rings and Multimode Fiber Optic Rings. Single-Mode Fiber uses a singular light path, facilitating long-distance communication with minimal signal loss, making it ideal for telecom and data centers. In contrast, Multimode Fiber Optic Rings allow multiple light paths, suitable for shorter distances and applications like local area networks (LANs). Both types are essential for high-speed data transmission, catering to different infrastructural needs and communication ranges.</p></p>
