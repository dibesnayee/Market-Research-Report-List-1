<p><p>The holographic diffraction grating market is segmented into two main types: plane type and concave type holographic gratings. Plane type holographic gratings are flat surfaces that disperse light into its constituent wavelengths, widely used in spectroscopy and telecommunications. In contrast, concave type holographic gratings have a curved surface that focuses light, enhancing efficiency in applications like lasers and optical instruments. Both types serve essential roles in scientific research and industrial processes, with distinct advantages tailored to specific optical needs.</p></p>
