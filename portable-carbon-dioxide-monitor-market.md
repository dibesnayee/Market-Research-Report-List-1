<p><p>The Portable Carbon Dioxide Monitor market is segmented into three main types: Mainstream, Sidestream, and Others. Mainstream carbon dioxide monitors directly measure CO2 levels using a sampling method that analyzes gas from the breathing zone. Sidestream monitors, on the other hand, draw breath samples through a tubing system for analysis, allowing for flexibility in patient positioning. The "Others" category encompasses various alternative technologies or applications not classified under mainstream or sidestream, expanding the market's diversity.</p></p>
