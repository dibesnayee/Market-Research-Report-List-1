<p><p>The transmission line stringing equipment market comprises various types, notably pullers and tensioners. Pullers are used to draw the conductor through the transmission towers, facilitating the installation process. Tensioners, on the other hand, manage and maintain the correct tension of the wire, ensuring it is appropriately positioned and secured. Other market categories may include equipment like reelers, swivels, and rollers, which assist in handling, guiding, and deploying conductors, thereby enhancing the efficiency and safety of stringing operations.</p></p>
