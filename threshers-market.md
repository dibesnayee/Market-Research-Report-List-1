<p><p>Threshers are classified into various market types based on their mechanisms and applications. The Spike-Tooth Type utilizes sharp spikes for efficient threshing of grains. The Axial Flow Type employs a continuous flow system to enhance productivity and reduce grain damage. The Hammer Mill Type uses hammers to break down materials, making it suitable for diverse crops. The Wire-Loop Type incorporates flexible wire loops for gentle threshing, preserving grain quality. Other types may include combinations or specialized designs catering to specific agricultural needs.</p></p>
