<p><p>The Color Selection Machine market encompasses various technologies for color detection and sorting. CCD technology employs charge-coupled devices that capture high-resolution color images for precise sorting. Infrared technology detects specific materials based on their infrared signatures, effectively separating items. X-ray technology analyzes the internal structure of materials, enabling sorting based on density and elemental composition, enhancing quality control in manufacturing and processing industries.</p></p>
