<p><p>The PC audio codec market is categorized into three main types: 1 DAC, 2 DACs, and Others. Systems with 2 DACs offer enhanced audio performance, especially in high-fidelity or gaming contexts, allowing for better separation of channels and improved sound clarity. The "Others" category includes specialized codecs that may integrate advanced features like surround sound, wireless capabilities, or support for multiple audio formats.</p></p>
