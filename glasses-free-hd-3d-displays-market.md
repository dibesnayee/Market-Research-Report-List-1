<p><p>Glasses-free HD 3D displays utilize several technologies to create depth perception without special eyewear. Light barrier technology uses a grid of barriers to guide light to specific angles, while lenticular lens technology employs a series of cylindrical lenses to direct images to the viewer’s eyes. Directional backlight systems illuminate pixels at varying angles for a 3D effect, and direct imaging involves multiple image layers producing depth. Other market segments may include emerging or hybrid technologies enhancing 3D experiences.</p></p>
